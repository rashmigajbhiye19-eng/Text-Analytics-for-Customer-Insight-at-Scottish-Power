Research Design

Methodology

Guided by the Saunders Research Onion, the study adopts an interpretivist, deductive, qualitative mono-method approach.

Layer 01
Research Philosophy — Interpretivism
Focuses on subjective experiences and contextual meanings within customer feedback, enabling depth over mere measurement.
InterpretivismQualitative
Layer 02
Research Approach — Deductive
Pre-established theories on customer sentiment and business outcomes guide the analytical framework, tested against real Trustpilot data.
DeductiveTheory-Testing
Layer 03
Strategy — Archival Research
Secondary Trustpilot data scraped via Octoparse. Avoids interviewer bias; captures organic, unsolicited customer expression.
OctoparseTrustpilotArchival
Layer 04
Data Analysis — NVivo & Voyant
Thematic coding in NVivo, word frequency & sentiment visualisations in Voyant. Systematic reduction of unstructured text into actionable insight.
NVivoVoyantThematic AnalysisSentiment Analysis
Layer 05
Time Horizon — Cross-Sectional
Snapshot of customer attitudes at a specific moment, ideal for evaluating the immediate effectiveness of service and marketing strategies.
Cross-SectionalSnapshot Analysis
Strategic Outcomes

Recommendations

Six data-driven interventions to transform customer feedback into competitive advantage.

01
🎓
Customer Service Training
Comprehensive programs targeting interpersonal skills, dispute resolution and empathetic communication to reduce negative service encounters.
02
💡
Transparent Pricing
Clear billing forms, proactive rate-change notices and itemised cost breakdowns to eliminate overpricing concerns and build trust.
03
📊
Feedback Culture
Continuous feedback systems using surveys, focus groups and online review monitoring to keep a live pulse on customer sentiment.
04
🔮
Predictive Analytics
Leverage historical data to anticipate customer needs, personalise communications and proactively prevent dissatisfaction before it escalates.
05
⚙️
Streamlined Feedback Mechanisms
Automated sentiment analysis and real-time customer experience tools to identify service gaps and respond with agility.
06
🛡️
Customer Care Initiative
Dedicated resolution teams to handle escalated complaints, converting negative experiences into demonstrations of exceptional service recovery.
Conclusion
"By utilising text analytics and structured frameworks, businesses can enhance customer experiences, optimise services, and achieve long-term profitability."
